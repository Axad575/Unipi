About Us

The Students Union of the University of Pisa in Tashkent is a key representative body that fosters student involvement and support within the university's branch in Uzbekistan. As an integral part of student life, the union serves as a platform where students can voice their concerns, ideas, and aspirations while engaging in academic, cultural, and social initiatives.
